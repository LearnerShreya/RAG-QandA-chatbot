
ICICI HOUSING LOANS – IMPORTANT NOTES

1. LOAN PRODUCTS:
- MaxMoney Home Loan:
    • Offers up to 30% higher eligibility on existing income.
    • Initial EMI starts lower and steps up over years. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
- SmartFix Home Loan:
    • Fixed interest rate for first 3 years.
    • Switches to floating rate from year 4 onwards. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
- Home Improvement Loan:
    • For renovations/repairs up to INR 50 lakhs.
    • Same interest rate as regular home loans. Repayment up to 15 years. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

2. PROPERTY ELIGIBILITY:
- Under-construction or ready properties, resale purchases,
- Self-construction and extension projects covered. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
- Loan-to-Value (LTV) up to 80% of property cost. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

3. TENURE:
- Home loan tenure up to 25 years.
- Borrower must be between 21 at sanction and ≤ 65 years at maturity. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

4. ELIGIBLE APPLICANTS:
- Salaried and self-employed Indian residents.
- NRI eligibility restricted to salaried profile. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

5. DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Common:
    • Completed application form + passport photo
    • ID, address, age proof
- Salaried:
    • Last 3 months salary slips, Form 16
    • 6 months bank statements
- Self-employed:
    • 2 years ITR and audited financials
    • 6 months business bank statements :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
- NRI-specific:
    • Valid passport + visa/OCI
    • NRI/OCI address proof
    • Overseas salary slips / bank statements
    • Power of Attorney document :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

6. ACCESSORIES & ADVANTAGES:
- Choice of fixed, floating, or hybrid interest rates.
- Free personal accident insurance and doorstep service. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
- Balance transfer and top-up loans available. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
- Lender-approved projects through Express Home Loan platform. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

7. NRI LOAN SPECIFICS:
- Eligible for purchase, construction, renovation, or land loans.
- Tenure of up to 30 years for salaried NRIs. :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
- Minimum overseas income: USD 42,000 (USA), AED 84,000 (GCC). :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}
- Home, Land, Improvement, LAP, Top-up, and Balance Transfer loans available. :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}

KEY INSIGHTS:
- **Product differentiation** (MaxMoney, SmartFix) aids varied applicant needs.
- **Flexible tenure & up to 80% LTV** help with affordability.
- **NRI inclusion** broadens applicant base, with clearly defined documentation.
- **Balance transfer/top-up** options enhance existing loan flexibility.

Home Loan Interest Rates (2024):

- Fixed Rate: 8.5% per annum
- Floating Rate: 8.2% to 9.0% per annum (varies with market)
- Hybrid Rate: 8.3% per annum for first 3 years, then floating

Interest rates may vary by applicant profile and market conditions. Always check with the lender for the latest rates.
